Privacy & data

Bandloop stores your name, instrument role, optional email and phone number. This information is shared only with members of your band — it is never sold, used for advertising, or shared with third parties.

Email and phone are used solely for band contact and account recovery. You can remove your data at any time by leaving all bands and asking your admin to delete your profile.

What we collect and why:

  • Name and instrument role — so your bandmates know who's who
  • Email — for account login and password recovery only
  • Phone number (optional) — displayed to your bandmates for contact
  • Calendar availability (optional) — busy/free times only, never event titles or details
  • Push notification tokens — to deliver notifications you've opted into

We do not use analytics tracking, advertising SDKs, or any form of data monetisation. Your band data stays between you and your bandmates.

For questions about your data, contact hello@bandloop.app.
