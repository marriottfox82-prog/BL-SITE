🎤

Gig Mode

Your setlist on stage, exactly how you need it. Full-screen view, large type, screen stays on. Tap to advance songs, see your personal notes alongside each one. No squinting, no fumbling — just play.

📋

Setlists

Build, reorder, and share setlists. Add band notes and personal notes per song. Import from Spotify, Chosic, CSV, or plain text. Export as PDF.

📅

Events & RSVPs

Schedule rehearsals and gigs. One-tap RSVP so everyone knows who's coming. Export to Google Calendar or Apple Calendar.

💡

Song suggestions

Any member can suggest songs. The whole band votes and comments. Convert winners straight into a setlist.

📆

Availability checker

Sync calendars from Google, iCloud, or Outlook. See when everyone's free at a glance. Find the best time for your next rehearsal.

🎙️

Recordings

Attach rehearsal recordings to setlists. Review performances and track progress over time.

👥

Multi-band

In multiple bands? Switch between them instantly. Each band has its own setlists, events, and members.

🔔

Push notifications

Know immediately when a rehearsal is added, a setlist changes, or someone suggests a new song. Customisable per category.

Privacy & data

Bandwolf stores your name, instrument role, optional email and phone number. This information is shared only with members of your band — it is never sold, used for advertising, or shared with third parties.

Email and phone are used solely for band contact and account recovery. You can remove your data at any time by leaving all bands and asking your admin to delete your profile.

What we collect and why:

    We do not use analytics tracking, advertising SDKs, or any form of data monetisation. Your band data stays between you and your bandmates.
