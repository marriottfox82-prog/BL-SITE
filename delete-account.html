Delete your Bandwolf account

Bandwolf — band management for working musicians

You can permanently delete your Bandwolf account and its associated data at any time, directly inside the app. You do not need to contact us.

How to delete your account

  1. Open the Bandwolf app.
  2. Go to Settings (the profile/settings tab).
  3. Tap Delete account.
  4. Confirm when prompted. This action is irreversible.

If you can no longer sign in and need your account removed, email hello@bandwolf.app from the address on the account and we will action the deletion.

What is deleted

Deleting your account permanently removes your profile (name and email), your membership of any bands, and the personal data tied to your account. Bands you administer and the content within them (setlists, songs, rehearsals) may remain available to the other members of those bands.

What is kept, and for how long

Content you soft-delete within the app (for example a removed setlist or song) is held in a recoverable trash for up to 60 days before it is permanently purged. Minimal records required for legal, security, or fraud-prevention purposes may be retained where required by law; otherwise account data is removed.
